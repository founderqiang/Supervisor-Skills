# Self-adversarial review

Run after synthesis, before presentation. The aim is not perfection; it is
catching the blind spots that feel fine while writing and get asked about
the moment someone else reads.

## Three retrospective questions

### 1. Starting over, would the brief change?

Return to the frozen research brief. Would what you now know change the
angle?

- No: CLEAR.
- Somewhat, core intact: add one line to the limitations.
- Substantially: frame-lock detected; seriously consider redoing the brief.

### 2. Any unexamined premises?

Is the survey built on assumptions never made explicit? Typical ones:
assuming higher accuracy means better; assuming English-dataset results
speak for other languages; assuming the current trend continues.

Found: add the qualification in the limitations or the affected section.

### 3. Are the conclusions evidence-driven or selection-driven?

For each core conclusion:

- Does counter-evidence exist, and was it presented?
- If only supporting works are cited: is that because none oppose, or
  because none were searched for?
- State the inverse of the claim: does anything support the inverse?

Selective citation found: search for the opposing side, present it in the
contested section.

## Perspective-omission check

- **Discipline**: did a cross-disciplinary topic get written from one
  discipline only?
- **Geography**: does the literature over-concentrate in a few regions'
  research groups?
- **Method**: did one method family (say, deep learning) crowd out the
  traditional approaches?
- **Criticism**: is the survey uniformly positive? Where are the failures,
  limits, and critics?

Omissions found: supplement in the open-problems or limitations sections.

## Concession rules

When this review finds a problem with a conclusion:

- Counter-evidence overturns it: rewrite the claim; downgrade to a
  conditional.
- Counter-evidence weakens it: keep the claim, add the qualification.
- A different-but-compatible perspective: present both in the contested
  section.
- A vague unease with no locatable counter-evidence: change nothing. Do not
  soften an evidence-backed judgment to perform humility.

## Output

No separate report. The output is the corrections themselves: revised
claims, added qualifications, supplemented contested sections. If all three
questions come back clean, proceed to presentation without manufacturing
self-criticism.
